Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how KaufAI collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use our website at https://kaufai.com, our API at https://api.kaufai.com, and our mobile applications on the Apple App Store and Google Play (collectively, the “Service”).

This policy satisfies requirements of the Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and third-party developer programs including eBay’s OAuth application requirements.

1. Data Collection

We collect the following categories of information when you use KaufAI:

Account information

  • Name, email address, and unique user identifier when you sign in with Google or Apple
  • Session tokens to keep you authenticated on the web app

Product and listing data

  • Photos and videos you upload for AI product identification
  • Listing titles, descriptions, prices, quantities, and categories
  • Sales records and marketplace names you enter or sync

Marketplace connection data

  • Mobile: OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored only on your device (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). They are sent to our server only for individual API requests you initiate.
  • Connection status and non-secret marketplace identifiers
  • Data returned by marketplace APIs within scopes you authorize (e.g., inventory, listings, seller ID)

Payment data

  • Billing metadata and transaction confirmations from Stripe. We do not store full credit card numbers or CVV codes.

Device and usage data

  • Device type, OS version, app version, IP address, and diagnostic logs for security and support
  • In-app preferences such as trial status and onboarding state

We do not sell your personal information or use advertising trackers.

2. Usage

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service, including authentication and account management
  • Analyze product images with AI to suggest listing content
  • Publish and manage listings on marketplaces you authorize
  • Process payments and service fees through Stripe
  • Send service-related communications and respond to support requests
  • Detect fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Improve the Service and comply with legal obligations

Legal basis (EEA/UK): Contract performance, legitimate interests in operating a secure service, and legal compliance where applicable under GDPR.

3. Sharing

We share information only as follows:

Service providers

  • OpenAI — AI image and text analysis. Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — Payment processing. Privacy Policy
  • Google / Apple — Sign-in authentication. Google  |  Apple
  • Cloud infrastructure — Hosting and database providers under data processing agreements

Marketplace platforms

When you publish a listing, we transmit listing data and authorized credentials to the relevant marketplace API (eBay, Etsy, Shopify, Allegro, etc.) solely to perform your requested action.

Legal and business events

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect rights and safety. In a merger or acquisition, your data may transfer with notice to you.

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties for marketing.

4. Marketplace & eBay API Data

KaufAI connects to marketplace APIs including eBay via OAuth. With your explicit consent, we may access seller identifiers, inventory, listing data, and order summaries permitted by approved scopes.

We use eBay and other marketplace data only to:

  • Create, update, publish, or end listings at your direction
  • Display sync status and results in the app
  • Support troubleshooting of marketplace errors you report

We do not use marketplace API data for unrelated advertising or resale to data brokers.

You may revoke access in KaufAI Settings or through the marketplace’s own third-party app permissions (eBay: Account Settings).

eBay is a trademark of eBay Inc. KaufAI is not affiliated with eBay. Use of eBay services is subject to eBay’s User Privacy Notice and API License Agreement.

5. Security

We protect your data using industry-standard measures, including:

  • HTTPS (TLS) encryption for all data in transit
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive server-side credentials
  • Secure device storage (Keychain / Keystore) for mobile OAuth tokens
  • Access controls and monitoring on production systems

No system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but work continuously to protect your information.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Product images are deleted within 90 days of upload or sooner when no longer needed
  • Marketplace tokens on mobile remain on your device until you disconnect or uninstall
  • Account deletion removes your data within 30 days, except where law requires longer retention

7. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Port your data in a machine-readable format (GDPR)
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Withdraw consent where processing is consent-based

California (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents may request disclosure or deletion of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

To exercise your rights, email support@kaufai.com with subject “Privacy Request.” We respond within 30 days. You may also delete your account in-app where available.

8. Cookies

Our web app uses:

  • Essential session cookies to keep you signed in
  • Local storage for preferences such as onboarding and consent
  • Mobile secure storage for OAuth tokens (not browser cookies)

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or cross-app behavioral analytics. You can manage cookies in your browser settings; disabling essential cookies may limit web app functionality.

9. Children’s Privacy

KaufAI is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact support@kaufai.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. International Users

Brazil (LGPD) / Canada (PIPEDA): Contact us to exercise applicable rights.

Data may be processed in the United States and other countries where our providers operate. We use appropriate safeguards for international transfers where required.
