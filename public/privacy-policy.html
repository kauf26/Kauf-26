1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following categories depending on how you use the Service:

Account and identity information

  • Sign-in credentials — When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and a unique account identifier provided by those services. We do not receive your Google or Apple password.
  • Profile information — Display name and email associated with your KAUF-AI account.
  • Session data — Encrypted session identifiers used to keep you logged in on the web app.

Product and listing information

  • Photos and videos — Images you capture or upload for AI product identification and listing generation.
  • Listing content — Titles, descriptions, prices, quantities, categories, and other fields you enter or approve before publishing.
  • Sales records — Sale amounts, marketplace names, and related notes you log in the app.

Marketplace connection information

  • OAuth tokens — On mobile, marketplace access and refresh tokens (e.g., eBay, Etsy, Shopify) are stored only on your device using the platform secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). We do not persist marketplace OAuth tokens in our database.
  • Connection metadata — Which marketplaces you have connected, connection status, and non-secret identifiers needed to route API requests.
  • Marketplace account data — When you authorize KAUF-AI to access a marketplace API, we may receive information permitted by the scopes you approve (see Section 4).

Payment information

  • Service fees are processed by Stripe. We receive transaction confirmations and billing metadata from Stripe. We do not collect or store full credit card numbers, CVV codes, or complete billing addresses on our servers.

Device and usage information

  • Device type, operating system, and app version — For compatibility, security, and support.
  • Log and diagnostic data — IP address, request timestamps, error logs, and API usage necessary to operate and secure the Service.
  • Preferences — Settings such as trial status, onboarding completion, and language preferences stored locally or in your account.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not use advertising trackers or sell your personal information.
  • We do not knowingly collect precise geolocation for advertising purposes.
  • We do not access your contacts, microphone, or SMS unless you explicitly grant a permission for a specific in-app feature that requires it.

4. Marketplace and eBay API Data

KAUF-AI integrates with third-party marketplace APIs, including eBay, to help you manage listings. This section describes our practices for marketplace-connected data, including data obtained through eBay’s OAuth authorization flow, as required by eBay’s developer program policies.

What we access

With your explicit authorization, KAUF-AI may access marketplace data permitted by the OAuth scopes you approve, which may include:

  • Your marketplace user or seller identifier
  • Inventory and listing data (titles, descriptions, prices, quantities, images, item IDs)
  • Order or transaction summaries needed to sync sales or inventory (where supported and authorized)
  • Account status information needed to confirm a valid connection

How we use marketplace data

  • To create, update, publish, or end listings at your direction
  • To display connection status and listing sync results in the app
  • To calculate service fees based on sales you record or sync
  • To troubleshoot errors you report related to marketplace publishing

We do not use eBay or other marketplace data for unrelated marketing, resale to third parties, or competitive intelligence beyond operating the Service for you.

How marketplace tokens are stored

  • Mobile app: OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored on your device in encrypted secure storage. They are sent to our server only for individual API operations you initiate and are not logged or cached server-side.
  • Web app: Marketplace authorization may use server-side OAuth with tokens encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption. Tokens are used only to perform marketplace actions you request.

Revoking access

You may disconnect a marketplace at any time in the app Settings. You may also revoke KAUF-AI’s access through the marketplace’s own account permissions page (for eBay: eBay Account Settings → Third-party app access).

eBay-specific notice

eBay and the eBay logo are trademarks of eBay Inc. KAUF-AI is not affiliated with or endorsed by eBay. Your use of eBay’s services is subject to eBay’s User Privacy Notice and applicable eBay terms. Data received from eBay APIs is handled in accordance with this Privacy Policy and eBay’s API License Agreement.

5. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption in transit — All data transmitted between your device, our servers, and third-party APIs uses HTTPS (TLS).
  • Encryption at rest — Sensitive data, including session secrets and encrypted marketplace credentials where applicable, is protected using industry-standard encryption.
  • Access controls — Limited personnel access to production systems on a need-to-know basis.
  • Secure mobile storage — Marketplace tokens on mobile are stored in iOS Keychain or Android Keystore via Expo SecureStore.
  • Monitoring — Logging and alerting for suspicious activity and service errors.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use a strong, unique password for any sign-in method and keep your device secure.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data — Retained while your account is active.
  • Product images — Retained only as long as needed to operate the Service; automatically deleted within 90 days of upload, or sooner when a product sells and you no longer need the listing.
  • Listing and sales records — Retained until you delete them or delete your account.
  • Marketplace tokens — On mobile, remain on your device until you disconnect the marketplace or uninstall the app. Server-side tokens, if any, are deleted when you disconnect or delete your account.
  • Logs — Retained for a limited period for security and troubleshooting, then deleted or anonymized.

You may delete your account at any time from Settings in the app or by contacting us. Account deletion permanently removes your account, listings, sales records, and associated personal data from our systems within 30 days, except where we must retain certain records for legal, tax, or fraud-prevention purposes.
