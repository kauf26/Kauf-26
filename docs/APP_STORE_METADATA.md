## Description (4000 characters max)

```
Turn product photos into polished marketplace listings in seconds. Kauf26 is the AI-powered listing assistant built for resellers, thrift flippers, and ecommerce sellers who list on eBay, Etsy, Shopify, and more.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Capture or upload a product photo with your iPhone camera
2. Kauf26's AI identifies the item, suggests a title, description, and price range
3. Review and edit the draft, then publish to your connected marketplaces

AI-POWERED IDENTIFICATION
Powered by OpenAI GPT-4o, Kauf26 analyzes your product images and generates professional listing copy tailored for each marketplace. No more staring at blank description fields — let AI do the heavy lifting while you stay in control of every publish.

MULTI-MARKETPLACE LISTING
Connect your seller accounts and publish from one app. Kauf26 supports major marketplaces including eBay, Etsy, Shopify, Amazon, Mercado Libre, and more. OAuth tokens stay on your device in the iOS Keychain — your credentials never sit on our servers.

BUILT FOR RESELLERS
Whether you source from thrift stores, estate sales, wholesale, or your own inventory, Kauf26 speeds up the listing workflow so you can list more products per day. Portrait-optimized for iPhone and iPad.

KEY FEATURES
• AI product identification from photos
• Auto-generated titles, descriptions, and price suggestions
• Multi-marketplace publishing (eBay, Etsy, Shopify, and more)
• Listing draft management and inventory tracking
• Sign in with Apple and Google
• Secure OAuth — marketplace tokens stored on-device only
• Business-category app optimized for sellers and resellers

PRIVACY FIRST
• No cross-app tracking
• No sale of personal data
• GDPR and CCPA compliant
• Full privacy policy at https://kauf-26.onrender.com/api/privacy

FREE TRIAL
New users receive a free trial period. After the trial, a small service fee applies per listing as disclosed in-app. No hidden charges.

WHO IS KAUF26 FOR?
• eBay and Etsy resellers
• Thrift and vintage sellers
• Shopify store owners adding inventory
• Side-hustle sellers scaling their listing volume
• Anyone who wants AI-assisted marketplace listings without juggling multiple apps

Download Kauf26 and transform how you list products online. List smarter. Sell faster.
```
